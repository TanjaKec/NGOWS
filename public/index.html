About

Social justice in a digital age

  • Who we are

    We are a not for profit social enterprise focussing on the objective of increasing the number of women involved in the field of data science. This is our primary mission; we do it to help address the gender imbalance in tech industries and to enable women to fulfil their potential regardless of their background.

  • Our Principles

    Our principles are founded on the four pillars of diversity, data literacy, transparency and inclusivity. We believe that everyone with the ability and determination should be able to flourish in the increasingly consequential, social and commercial world of DS.

  • Our Courses

    Our courses are specifically structured to help address some of the learning obstacles that women/non binary individuals may confront when entering an emerging field such as data science, where women are a minority.

  • Our Goal

    Sister Analyst aims to empower women in an underrepresented tech industry by developing technical as well as gender specific skills to achieve equitable representation.
